attribute selectors

this is a paragraph without the title attribute

p[title] selectors all paragraphs containing a title attribute.

p[title~="paragraph"] selects all paragraphs with a title attribute contining the word, paragraph

p[title="#4 paragraph"] selects all paragraphs with a title attribute containig the exact text, #4 paragraph. matches r case-sensetive and must match letter-for-letter including whitespace.
